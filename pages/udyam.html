Establishment of the MSME-Development & Facilitation Office (DFO) at Dehradun

📌 Background

The establishment of the MSME-Development & Facilitation Office (DFO) at Dehradun is a significant step toward strengthening the MSME ecosystem in Uttarakhand. As of June 2025, Uttarakhand has more than 3.56 lakh registered MSMEs spread across the Garhwal and Kumaon regions.

Prior to the establishment of the Dehradun office, the entire state was served by a single MSME-DFO located in Haldwani. This arrangement created considerable challenges for enterprises located in the Garhwal region, particularly those in and around Dehradun, Haridwar, and other major industrial areas.

⚠ Challenges Faced

The location of the sole DFO in Haldwani resulted in accessibility and coordination issues for MSMEs in Garhwal. Entrepreneurs and industry representatives frequently faced long travel distances to access government schemes, support services, and official consultations.

The situation also affected effective coordination with state government departments situated in Dehradun, the state capital. Industry associations and stakeholders submitted formal requests for a dedicated MSME office in the Garhwal region.

🏛 Establishment of DFO Dehradun

In response to these demands, the Government of India established the MSME-DFO at Dehradun. The office was inaugurated on 14 February 2024 by the Hon’ble Minister of MSME.

Dehradun was selected because it is the administrative capital of Uttarakhand and houses the State Secretariat, Legislative Assembly, and Directorate of Industries, enabling better coordination and implementation of MSME programmes.

🏢 Infrastructure Support

To facilitate the functioning of the new office, a temporary office space of approximately 3,000 square feet was requested from the State Government, while around five acres of land has been proposed for a permanent office complex.

As an interim arrangement, the Government of Uttarakhand allotted three rooms within the Uttarakhand Khadi and Village Industries Board (KVIB) campus at Thano Road, Bhopal Pani, Dehradun.

👥 Proposed Staffing Structure

  • DFO Dehradun: 18 Posts
  • Haldwani Branch Office: 14 Posts
  • Subject to approval by MSME Headquarters.

🚀 Expected Benefits

  • Improved accessibility of MSME services in Garhwal region.
  • Better coordination with State Government departments.
  • Faster implementation of MSME schemes and programmes.
  • Promotion of entrepreneurship and industrial growth.
  • Strengthened economic development across Uttarakhand.
